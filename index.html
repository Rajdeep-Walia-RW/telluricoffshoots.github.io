Business Structure & Services Provided

  • Industries Served

    Precision Components: We source agent to source custom made precision components for Automobile, Aviation, Plumbing, Lighting, Electrical, Firearms and many more industries.

  • Vendor Network

    We have a pan India network of qualified vendors for various processes serving the above industries. Competences available:

    Machining Metal Injection Moulding (MIM) Investment Casting Injection Moulding Sheet Metal components Forging Fabrication Laser Cutting Electrical & Electronics Pressure Die Casting of Aluminium, Zinc and Magnesium Surface Finishing operations like Plating, Powder Coating, Heat treatment, Nitriding, Anodizing etc.

  • Raw Materials Used

    Stainless Steels Alloy Steels Aluminium Brass Zinc Magnesium

  • Quality Assurance & Compliances

    Apart from the inhouse processes and SOP of the vendor we need to have a third-party inspection / audit agency appointed as per your requirement. They remain your resources to be deployed as needed. We will coordinate with them to ensure seamless inspections and reporting.

    You may also look at the option of deploying a suitable Quality engineer on rolls to take care of all inspections.

  • Logistics

    We develop vendors close to the coastal areas so that inland transport cost is saved to the extent possible. Vendors can offer ex-works, FOB or CIF as per your requirement.

  • Business Structure & Services Provided

    We only act as buyer’s agent and transparently charge our fee and expenses payable by buyer. We expect a detailed RFQ from buyer providing the complete engineering drawings, 3d models, EAUs and expected MOQs. Based on that suitable vendor are identified. RFQ is floated to multiple vendors. Quotes are submitted after suitable whetting to ensure they are complete in all aspects. The entire cycle of RFQ to dispatch of material and payments is handled by us. Suitable reports are submitted at appropriate frequency to ensure you are updated of developments.
